About Dr. Olivia Makanga

I believe in a holistic, patient-centered approach to healthcare, focusing on your long-term well-being.

Committed to Excellence in Healthcare

As a dedicated General Practitioner, my passion is to provide comprehensive and compassionate healthcare to individuals and families in the Pretoria community. I believe that effective medicine goes beyond treating symptoms; it involves building a trusting relationship with each patient to understand their unique health journey and goals.

My approach is rooted in holistic patient-centered care, focusing on preventive health, accurate diagnostics, and long-term wellness. I take the time to listen, educate, and empower you to make informed decisions about your health.
