Our Services

Providing essential and compassionate primary healthcare for you and your family in Pretoria.

General Consultations

Your first point of contact for acute illnesses, routine check-ups, and managing general health concerns for all family members.

Preventative Health

Proactive health screenings, lifestyle advice, and vaccinations aimed at preventing illness and promoting long-term well-being.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing support and management plans for conditions like diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and arthritis to improve your quality of life.

Child & Adolescent Health

Comprehensive paediatric care including routine check-ups, growth monitoring, and essential immunisations for your children.

Women's and Men's Health

Dedicated health services addressing specific needs for both women (e.g., Pap smears) and men (e.g., prostate health).

Minor Procedures & Referrals

Offering minor in-office procedures, medical certificates, and expert referrals to specialists when further care is needed.
